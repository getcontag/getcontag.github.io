Tagging and in-call client context.

You can tag your clients/contacts to various categories. Get in-call reminders with all the latest and important information about the client. Never miss the important topic to discuss during call.

Efficent on-time followup

Set up follow-up reminders right after the call. This gives you the option to connect/follow-up with clients without forgetting.

Handy notes about all clients.

Make call summaries right after all your calls. CONTAG provides you with a very intuitive timeline interface to know all the discussions with your client till date.

Better Management

Tag multiple contacts smartly and quickly. Contag lets you tag your clients/contacts to various custom-made categories! Keep things organized, prioritize efficiently and stay ahead of the game.

Accessibility

Forget about forgetting! Notes and indispensable data are presented to you in real time. Say goodbye to data entry! A customer database is created for you, automatically logging calls, texts to each contact card. Contag is a unique app that truly adapts to your workflow and automatically builds a smart contact book, thereby presenting crucial customer information exactly when you need them.

All in one Place

Contag provides you with a very intuitive timeline interface to know about all the interactions with your client till date. Your calls, texts, reminders and notes are ordered chronologically, right where you need them

Personalization

Contag has been built keeping the end-user in mind. So you have the ultimate power to customize all your tags to suit your needs. This highly customizable, color-coded tag system drastically boosts productivity.

Plan and Deliver

We know what's really important for you on any day. So we present you with a concise agenda - the tasks and other interactions which are of utmost importance for the day.

Maximum Efficiency

Won't it be really efficient if you could immediately recollect your past conversation with a customer when you receive a call from him/her? Contag precisely delivers on that. It presents you with the latest note and follow-up which you added for that customer in the call screen, just before you pick up the call. This unique feature clearly eliminates all the hassles from the customer's perspective and stimulates customer satisfaction.

Save Time

The best part about Contag is, that you never have to explicitly go to our app to update your game-changing data. We know your crucial needs and therefore we present your vital information at the right time. Contag saves plenty of your time because you can update your customer information right after you end your call automatically.
